Explanation:

Trend is the short-term average market price compared to the long-term average market price of a trading pair.


RSI is the Relative Strength Index of a trading pair. A value of 50 is neutral with above a uptrend, and below a downtrend.


Beta is the relative volatility of a trading pair compared to an index. In this case, the index is the grouping (ZAR, USDC, USDT).


And lastly ATR% is the Average True Range divided by the SMA.
